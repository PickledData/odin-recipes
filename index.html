Pickles Recipe

Description: Picle all sorts with vinegar, including cucumbers!

  • pickle cucumbers
  • salt
  • water
  • clean jar
  • herbs
  1. Wash pickle cucumbers thoroughly
  2. Add 1:1 water to vinegar
  3. Add salt to solution and reach boil
  4. Add boiling solution into glass jar and add herbs
